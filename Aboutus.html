BAKER'S KITCHEN

About Us

Baker's kitchen is a bakery that was started based off of my love for baking and providing clean yet affordable snacks for people which turned into a passsion for creating delicious treats. We are located along Gayaza road eight miles off Kampala P.O.Box1234,Wakiso.

Baker's kitchen offers desserts, pastries and all baked products which include all types of cakes, croissants, pies, tarts, doughnuts, danishes,scones,eclairs, sweet rools, cinnamon rolls, tiramisu etc with desserts as our specialities. Our committment is to use high quality ingredients and making every customer's experience delightful as well as give back to the society.

Our prices are affordable and offer discount rates on our goods during special occasions like holidays,anniversaries or seasonal promotions to attract more customers and give back to our daily customers.

We also make special and customized baked products basing on what the customer wants.

For placing orders and making deliveries contact us on the numbers in the help page as well as reaching out to us using our social media platforms and the deliveries come at a reasonable fee or free of charge depending on the offers available. We are open on all week days(Monday to Friday) and weekends(Saturday and Sunday) and receive orders on holidays or special days as well.

Mission

To delight our customers with delicious freshly baked goods that bring joy to every bite.

Vision

To become the go to bakery for our community, known for our exceptional quality, creative flavors and warm customer service.
